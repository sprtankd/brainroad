Why train your brain?

// the science-ish bit
25 m

Stopping distance at 90 km/h

With a one-second reaction time, your car travels 25 metres before you even touch the brake. Halve your reaction time and you halve that distance. That's why Brake! exists.

325+

Road signs in Sweden

Swedish signs use a yellow background to stand out in snow. On the theory test — and in real traffic — they must be read in a fraction of a second. Sign Master and The Scout build that automaticity.

⅓

Of accidents happen at intersections

The right-hand rule applies everywhere without other signs — even in car parks. The Intersection game drills your decision-making until it's in your spinal cord, not your conscious deliberation.

A brain-training game — does not replace official theory materials or the Swedish Transport Agency test. Drive safely. 🦌
